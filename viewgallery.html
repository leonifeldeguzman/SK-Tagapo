May 18, 2025

The SK of Brgy. Tagapo officially opened its much-anticipated SK Summer League with an opening ceremony

Athletes, sports enthusiasts, and community members gathered to mark the start of the tournament, which will feature weeks of spirited competition and foster camaraderie among the youth.

The league is still ongoing, with various teams battling it out on the courts, promoting sportsmanship and active living within the barangay.

April 28, 2025

Barangay Tagapo SK Hosts Vital Teenage Pregnancy and HIV Awareness Forum, Offers Testing and Supplies

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) ng Barangay Tagapo, in collaboration with the Barangay Health Center, held a successful "Teenage Pregnancy and HIV Awareness" forum last April 28, 2025.

Observing HIV Prevention Month, the event not only provided crucial information but also offered free testing services and distributed condoms and contraceptives, empowering Tagapo's youth with vital health education and resources.

April 29, 2025

Alyssa Jane Roxas and Matthew Hernandez Crowned Ginoo at Rosas Tagapo 2025

Barangay Tagapo celebrated beauty, talent, and community spirit on April 29 with the highly anticipated "Ginoo at Rosas Tagapo" pageant.

After an evening of impressive displays, Alyssa Jane Roxas and Matthew Hernandez emerged as the night’s victors, taking home the coveted titles of Rosas Tagapo and Ginoo Tagapo, respectively. The event highlighted the grace and charisma of Tagapo’s youth, delighting the local audience.

January 19, 2025

Barangay Tagapo Swept Sikhayan Festival 2025 Street Dance, Winning Grand Prize and Best in Costume

On January 19, 2025, Barangay Tagapo made history, securing their third consecutive championship at the Sikhayan Festival 2025 Street Dance Competition.
They also earned the prestigious "Best in Costume" award. This dominant showing cemented Tagapo's legacy in the festival's history.

December 08, 2024

The ML tournament by SK Tagapo ended with excitement, teamwork, and strong support from young players.

The Sanggunian Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Tagapo successfully concluded its Mobile Legends: Bang Bang tournament on December 16, 2024. The event, which drew numerous young enthusiasts, provided a valuable platform for mobile gaming talents within the community.

The tournament not only showcased impressive strategic prowess and individual skill but also fostered camaraderie and healthy competition among the participating teams. The SK expressed its commitment to continuing such initiatives that engage the youth in productive and skill-developing activities.
